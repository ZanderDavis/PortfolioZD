Cam Trainer Overview

CamTrainer was a four person group project that I worked on during my senior year at Missouri State University. CamTrainer allows users to start working out and perform exercises with correct form. CamTrainer analyzes the user and, based on their experience level, adjusts the strictness of form evaluation, helping users achieve the proper technique. We currently offer two workouts: push-ups and squats. For CamTrainer, we used React and Node.js as the foundation for our program and JavaScript as our primary programming language. We also utilize MySQL for our database to store all of our data.

My Contributions

My contributions to CamTrainer focused on the frontend and backend server communication, enabling users to: Create, delete, and update their profiles. Authenticate their accounts securely. Ensure that no two users share the same email address.
